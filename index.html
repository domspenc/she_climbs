Rock climbing, or simply 'climbing', is a sport where people will climb up, across, or down a natural or artificial rock face.

This sport has multiple disciplines including bouldering, sport climbing, traditional (or trad) climbing, and deep water soloing. Climbers solve 'problems' as they progress through a route, and have to figure out optimum body positioning and movements or 'moves' to successfully 'send' or finish a route without falling.

People have been climbing for centuries, and it has most recently become a sport at the Olympic Games! There are a plethora of incredible female climbers that inspire people all over the world. These women are record breakers, and send some of the most difficult routes known to the climbing world. They are certainly paving the way for the future of climbing!
